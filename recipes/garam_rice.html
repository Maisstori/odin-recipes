Garam Rice Recipe

Description

This is a wonderful recipe that I learned to make when I still wasn't able to get a job.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Rice
  • Garam Masala
  • Salt
  • MSG
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Pour oil into a your preferred cooking container
  2. Pour the rice into the container
  3. Pour the garam masala into the rice
  4. Cook until the rice gets the garam masala colour
  5. et voila!
